Delicious Pancakes Recipe

These pancakes are fluffy, easy to make, and perfect for breakfast. Follow this simple recipe to enjoy homemade pancakes in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, and melted butter together.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear, then flip and cook the other side.
  6. Serve warm with syrup or your favorite toppings.
